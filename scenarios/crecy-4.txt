Scenario: Crecy-4.sav
====================

Max number of players: 4
Nations: England, France, Russia and Turkey
Tech: All players got the same technology


Situation between England and France:
=====================================

The battle at Crécy and Calais in France in 1345.  

King Edward III has established an army in France, his main goal is
to eliminate "Fillip of Valois" who named himself: King of France.

Situation between Russia and Turkey:
====================================
Timur Lenk, the tyrant of Russia, has conquered Afghanistan. His army has
killed everything on their way and right now he has set his eyes on Ankara.
Bayezid of Turkey has noticed the enemy and has established an army of
85,000 against Timur's 145,000 men.
